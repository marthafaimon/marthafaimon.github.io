Professional Portfolio

Welcome to my online Portfolio. My name is Martha Faimon, and I am in my first year as an elementary library media specialist. I look forward to sharing my credentials and experiences.

Goals of This Website

The purpose of this website is to share about my professional career, as well as my current projects and studies towards my graduate degree. I will be including my projects and experiences, including information about my studies in learning technologies and library experience. I will also discuss my personal philosophy and reflection on how I came to form that philosophy. I hope that visiting this website will give the reader a better understanding of my education and professional life, as well as how I got to this point in my career. I would also like this website to serve as a place I can update as my career and learning continues. I will continue doing projects and having experiences during my time in graduate school and would appreciate having a place to update and share as I continue to learn. I hope readers learn more about my journey and experience!
